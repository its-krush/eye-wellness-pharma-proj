Our Science, Your Sight

AquaDrop was founded on the principle that eye wellness should be backed by rigorous clinical data but delivered with the gentleness of nature. Our laboratory specializes in ophthalmic hydration science, dedicated to advancing the field of preventive eye care.

We leverage advanced Hyaluronic Acid (HA) matrices to create a longer-lasting tear film, reducing the need for frequent re-application and improving patient quality of life in increasingly digital environments. Each formulation undergoes extensive biocompatibility testing and clinical trials to ensure both safety and efficacy.

Our research team collaborates with leading ophthalmologists and optometrists worldwide to develop solutions that address the evolving needs of modern eye care. From digital eye strain to post-surgical recovery, we're committed to delivering precision-engineered products that make a real difference in people's lives.
